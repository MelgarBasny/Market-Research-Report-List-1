<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Under-display Fingerprint Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Capacitive Fingerprint Module</li><li>Optical Fingerprint Module</li><li>Ultrasonic Fingerprint Module Module</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The under-display fingerprint module market comprises three primary types: capacitive, optical, and ultrasonic fingerprint modules. Capacitive modules utilize electrical signals to capture fingerprint patterns by measuring conductivity differences. Optical modules use light to illuminate the finger, capturing a detailed image of the fingerprint for recognition. Ultrasonic modules employ sound waves to create a 3D map of the fingerprint, detecting even the minutest features. Each type offers distinct advantages, catering to varying security and performance needs in devices like smartphones.</p></p>
