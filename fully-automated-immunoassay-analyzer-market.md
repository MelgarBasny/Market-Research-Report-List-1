<p><p>The Fully Automated Immunoassay Analyzer market comprises three primary types: Chemiluminescence Analysis, Fluorescent Immunoassay, and Radioimmunoassay. Chemiluminescence Analysis utilizes light emission from chemical reactions for detection, offering high sensitivity and speed. Fluorescent Immunoassay employs fluorescent labels that emit light upon excitation, allowing precise quantification. Radioimmunoassay, though less common today, uses radioactive isotopes for detection, providing high specificity. Each type plays a crucial role in diagnostic testing, enabling efficient and accurate analysis of various biomarkers in clinical laboratories.</p></p>
