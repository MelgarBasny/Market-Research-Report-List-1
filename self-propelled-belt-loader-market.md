<p><p>The self-propelled belt loader market encompasses various transmission types. Hydraulic transmission relies solely on hydraulic systems, offering smooth control and high torque, ideal for heavy loads. Power transmission incorporates a variety of energy transfer methods, including electrical or mechanical means, ensuring effective movement of the belt loader. Each type is designed to enhance performance in airport logistics and material handling applications, optimizing efficiency and reliability.</p></p>
