<p><p>The Metal Neurovascular Stent Market is primarily categorized into two types: Carotid artery stents and Intracranial stents. Carotid artery stents are designed to support the carotid arteries, improving blood flow and preventing strokes caused by arterial blockages. Intracranial stents, on the other hand, are used within the brain's vascular network to treat conditions like aneurysms or blockages, ensuring proper blood circulation. Both types are essential for enhancing neurovascular health and addressing critical cerebrovascular conditions.</p></p>
