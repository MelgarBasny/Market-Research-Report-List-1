<p><p>The high-performance glass fiber market includes three primary types: Continuous Fiber, Fixed Length Fiber, and Glass Wool. Continuous Fiber consists of long strands that provide superior strength and flexibility, typically used in composite materials. Fixed Length Fiber, cut into specific sizes, offers ease of handling and processing, making it suitable for various applications. Glass Wool, made from fine glass fibers, is primarily used for insulation due to its excellent thermal properties. Collectively, these fibers cater to diverse industrial and construction needs.</p></p>
