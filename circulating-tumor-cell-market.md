CTC Enrichment focuses on isolating tumor cells from blood samples, enhancing their purity for further study. CTC Detection involves identifying and quantifying these cells to assess their presence and levels in circulation. CTC Analysis encompasses the evaluation of the isolated cells' characteristics, such as genetic and phenotypic profiling, to gain insights into tumor behavior and treatment response.</p></p>
