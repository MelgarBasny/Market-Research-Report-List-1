<p><p>Pilot Operated Safety Valve (POSV) markets are categorized into two types: closed type and non-closed type. Closed type POSVs maintain the valve position under normal conditions, preventing fluid from escaping during system operation. They are typically used in applications requiring high reliability and minimal leakage. In contrast, non-closed type POSVs allow for some fluid discharge and are utilized in scenarios where pressure relief is crucial, providing safety while accommodating potential fluctuations in pressure within the system.</p></p>
