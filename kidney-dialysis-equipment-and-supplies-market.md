<p><ul><li>Hemodialysis</li><li>Peritoneal Dialysis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The kidney dialysis equipment and supplies market encompasses two primary types: hemodialysis and peritoneal dialysis. Hemodialysis involves using a machine and a filter, or dialyzer, to clean the blood outside the body. It typically requires access to the bloodstream via fistula, graft, or catheter. In contrast, peritoneal dialysis utilizes the lining of the abdominal cavity as a natural filter, introducing a dialysis solution through a catheter. Each method addresses kidney failure through different mechanisms, catering to various patient needs and settings.</p></p>
