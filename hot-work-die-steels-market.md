<p><p>The hot-work die steel market consists of various types designed for high-temperature applications. Hammer forging dies are utilized for shaping metal using a hammering process, offering durability and resistance to thermal fatigue. Hot extrusion dies facilitate the shaping of materials by forcing them through a die under high temperature and pressure, ensuring precise dimensions. Die casting dies are employed in manufacturing, allowing for the mass production of metal components by pouring molten metal into a mold, ensuring excellent surface finish and dimensional accuracy.</p></p>
