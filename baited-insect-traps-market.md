<p><p>The baited insect traps market encompasses various types designed for pest control, including bait stations, granular baits, and gel baits. Bait stations are enclosed containers distributing attractive pheromones or poison to lure insects effectively. Granular baits consist of powdery substances that attract pests while providing a toxic effect upon ingestion. Gel baits, often viscous, adhere to surfaces and release attractants, ensuring prolonged exposure for insects. Together, these methods enhance pest management strategies in both residential and commercial settings.</p></p>
