<p><p>The automotive lamps market is primarily segmented into three types: halogen, HID (High-Intensity Discharge), and LED (Light Emitting Diode) lighting. Halogen lighting offers affordability and simplicity, providing a warm light but with limited lifespan. HID lighting delivers brighter illumination and energy efficiency, making it popular for high-performance vehicles. LED lighting is gaining prominence due to its longevity, lower power consumption, and versatility in design, providing enhanced visibility and aesthetics. Each type caters to different consumer preferences and automotive applications.</p></p>
