<p><p>The bipolar pacing device market includes two primary types: temporary pacing electrodes and bipolar temporary pacing catheters. Temporary pacing electrodes are designed for short-term cardiac support, providing electrical stimulation to maintain heart rhythm during surgeries or acute heart conditions. Bipolar temporary pacing catheters, on the other hand, offer a flexible and minimally invasive solution for temporary cardiac pacing via both bipolar leads, enhancing precision and reducing the risk of complications, thus ensuring effective heart rate management in critical situations.</p></p>
