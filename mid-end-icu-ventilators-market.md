<p><p>The mid-end ICU ventilators market comprises two main types: invasive and non-invasive ventilation. Invasive ventilation involves the use of endotracheal tubes or tracheostomies to deliver air directly into the lungs, making it suitable for patients with severe respiratory distress. Non-invasive ventilation utilizes masks or similar devices to support breathing without the need for intubation, making it applicable for less critical cases. Both methods aim to improve patient outcomes by enhancing respiratory function in various clinical settings.</p></p>
