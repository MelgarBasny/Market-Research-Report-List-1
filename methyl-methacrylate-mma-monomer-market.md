<p><p>The Methyl Methacrylate (MMA) monomer market comprises various production methods. The ACH method utilizes acetone cyanohydrin as a precursor, resulting in high purity MMA. The ethylene method uses ethylene and methacrylic acid to produce MMA, focusing on cost-effectiveness and sustainability. Each method has distinct advantages and applications, catering to diverse industrial needs in the production of polymers and resins.</p></p>
