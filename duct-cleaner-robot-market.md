<p><p>The duct cleaner robot market comprises three primary types: round ducts robots, square ducts robots, and universal robots. Round ducts robots are specifically designed for cleaning circular ductwork, utilizing flexible designs to navigate tight spaces. Square ducts robots cater to square-shaped ventilation systems, providing efficient cleaning solutions for this geometry. Universal robots combine features to operate in both round and square ducts, offering versatility for diverse cleaning requirements. Each type serves distinct market needs based on duct shapes and configurations.</p></p>
