<p><p>The LED Diffuse Reflective Photoelectric Sensors market can be divided into two primary types based on the signal output: digital signal and analog signal. Digital signal sensors provide a binary output, indicating whether an object is detected or not, making them ideal for applications requiring clear on/off signaling. In contrast, analog signal sensors offer varying output levels based on the distance or intensity of the detected object, enabling more precise measurements and feedback in applications like automation and robotics.</p></p>
