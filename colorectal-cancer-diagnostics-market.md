<p><p>The colorectal cancer diagnostics market encompasses various testing methods to detect and monitor the disease. FOB (Fecal Occult Blood) tests identify hidden blood in stool, indicating potential cancer. CTC (Circulating Tumor Cell) tests analyze cells shed from tumors into the bloodstream for early detection and monitoring. CEA (Carcinoembryonic Antigen) tests measure levels of a specific protein, helping assess treatment effectiveness and recurrence risk. Other tests may include imaging and genetic profiling, enhancing diagnostic accuracy and patient management.</p></p>
