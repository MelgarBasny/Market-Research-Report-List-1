<p><ul><li>Six Side</li><li>Eight Side</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automatic rotary pulp molding machines are categorized into six-side and eight-side types based on their molding capabilities. Six-side machines can produce products from six different molds simultaneously, making them suitable for moderate production volumes and simpler designs. In contrast, eight-side machines offer higher efficiency with the ability to mold from eight different sides, allowing for more complex shapes and larger production outputs. Both types cater to various industries, including packaging, tableware, and construction materials.</p></p>
