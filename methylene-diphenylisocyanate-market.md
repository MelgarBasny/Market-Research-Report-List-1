<p><p>The Methylene Diphenylisocyanate (MDI) market comprises three main types: Polymeric MDI, Pure MDI, and Modified MDI. Polymeric MDI, a mixture of oligomers, is widely used in producing rigid foams, coatings, and adhesives due to its excellent properties. Pure MDI consists of a single isocyanate compound, primarily utilized in high-performance applications like elastomers. Modified MDI, which includes additives to enhance specific characteristics, is often employed in specialized applications requiring improved processing or performance, such as flexible foams and sealants.</p></p>
