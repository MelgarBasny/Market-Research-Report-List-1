<p><ul><li>Single-Spark</li><li>Multi-Spark</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive spark coil market is divided into two main types: single-spark and multi-spark coils. Single-spark coils ignite the air-fuel mixture in one burst per cycle, typically used in standard ignition systems. Multi-spark coils, on the other hand, produce multiple sparks during each ignition cycle, improving combustion efficiency and enhancing engine performance, especially in high-performance vehicles. This distinction influences substitutive technologies, with multi-spark systems increasingly favored for their ability to optimize power output and fuel efficiency.</p></p>
