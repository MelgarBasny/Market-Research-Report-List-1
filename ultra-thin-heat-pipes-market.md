<p><p>The ultra-thin heat pipes market encompasses two primary types: round heat pipes and flat heat pipes. Round heat pipes are cylindrical and are commonly used in applications requiring efficient thermal management in limited spaces, such as electronics cooling. Flat heat pipes, on the other hand, have a planar design, making them suitable for applications where space constraints are critical, like in lightweight devices and advanced electronics. Both types leverage phase change and capillary action to optimize heat transfer in compact environments.</p></p>
